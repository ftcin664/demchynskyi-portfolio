Better Health Hackathon on CodeforCovid19

The application addresses the shortage of RT-PCR testing kits by providing rapid and reliable AI screening, improving productivity for health professionals and aiding in remote areas with limited specialized physicians. It uses a deep convolutional neural network (DNN) to classify images as either normal, pneumonia, or COVID-19, with high accuracy rates of 93.3% for X-rays and 99.1% for CT scans. Data augmentation techniques and a ResNet architecture with lightweight PEPX design patterns enhance model performance. Additionally, the tool provides activation maps to help users visualize chest abnormalities like consolidation or ground-glass opacities.
